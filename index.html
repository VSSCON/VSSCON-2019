MESSAGE FROM SECRETARIAT

Greetings from VSSUT, Burla! After the adulation that previous editions of VSSUT MUNs have received in the MUN circuit, VSSUT is proud to present VSSUT Conference of Nations, 2019. For all the enthusiastic people out there, VSSCON’19 will be a platform for interested people to talk and debate on global pressing issues, make their voices and opnions heard and build strong relations with fellow delegates for a brighter insight into the future of leadership that awaits! It will be one of the biggest celebrations of the power of expression which is bestowed upon every delegate! Come, be a part of this display of diplomacy to find the epicenter for world solutions. The summit will be a congregation of highly intelligent and seasoned MUNners which will bring about the best, on record. To all aspiring delegates, you’ll be exposed to a gamut of world issues and get the warm welcome you deserve! Participate in this socio-economic educational multitude, a one of its kind, simulation of the United Nations and pace your way towards the pinnacle of diplomacy!
